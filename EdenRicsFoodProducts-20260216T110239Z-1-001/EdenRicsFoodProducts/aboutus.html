About Us

Our business, Edenric’s Food Product was established during the pandemic,
a time when many individuals faced job losses. In response to the pandemic
and the influence of farming, Edenric’s created a food business where products are a 100%
organic and does not contain any chemicals. We aim to cater to a wide range of consumers,
especially health-conscious teenagers, the elderly, and children.
